To: my love
In a world that rushes past too fast,
Gaurav, you became my pause.
Somewhere between your laughter
and the way you say my name,
I found a place that feels like home.
Since 13 December 2024,
love has felt softer, warmer, and more real—
like my heart finally knew
where it belongs.
Loving you isn’t loud or hurried,
it’s gentle, steady, and true.
It’s choosing you in a thousand small ways,
every single day.
So with a heart full of hope
and feelings deeper than these words,
